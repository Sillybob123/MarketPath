Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 12, 2025

Yair Ben-Dor ("we," "us," or "our") operates the MarketPath mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we handle information in the App.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. The MarketPath app was designed from the ground up to be a safe, educational environment that respects your privacy.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you. The MarketPath app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal data such as your name, email address, or location.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores certain data locally on your device to save your progress. This includes:

  • Your virtual portfolio value and holdings.
  • Your completed learning modules and achievements.
  • Your current experience points (XP) and user level.

This information is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. If you delete the App, this data will be permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

MarketPath does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. As we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
